**Phenixio Studio** | Last updated: March 9, 2026

## English

### Who we are
Simulacrum: AI & Tracker is developed by **Phenixio Studio** (Sylvain De Luca, Toulouse, France).
Contact: phenixio.studio@gmail.com

### What data we collect and why

**Game statistics (PvE mode only — games over 60 seconds)**

| Data | Purpose | Stored |
|------|---------|--------|
| Game outcome (victory / defeat / abandon / draw) | Display your personal statistics | Supabase servers (EU) |
| Game duration, turn count | Display your personal statistics | Supabase servers (EU) |
| Final life points (player & AI) | Display your personal statistics | Supabase servers (EU) |
| AI opponent name & difficulty | Display your personal statistics | Supabase servers (EU) |
| Spells played / countered, max damage | Display your personal statistics | Supabase servers (EU) |
| Player names (as entered in-game) | Display participant list in history | Supabase servers (EU) |

PvP games are never sent to any server.

**Support system**

| Data | Purpose | Stored |
|------|---------|--------|
| Anonymous session ID (Supabase Auth) | Identify your support session without requiring an account | Supabase servers (EU) |
| Device fingerprint (SHA256 hash) | Anti-spam: limit support ticket submissions to 1 per 12h | Supabase servers (EU) |
| Support ticket content (subject, message) | Answer your support request | Supabase servers (EU) |
| Email address (optional, only if you provide it) | Allow us to reply to your ticket | Supabase servers (EU) |

**We do NOT collect:**
- Your real name or identity
- Your location or GPS data
- PvP game data
- Any advertising or tracking identifiers
- Data from third parties

### How long we keep your data
- Support tickets: deleted after 12 months
- Device fingerprint records: deleted after 30 days
- Anonymous session: deleted after 12 months of inactivity

### Who has access to your data
Your data is stored on **Supabase** infrastructure (EU region) and is never sold or shared with third parties.
Supabase privacy policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

### Your rights (GDPR)
You have the right to:
- **Access** your data
- **Delete** your data
- **Object** to processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at: phenixio.studio@gmail.com
We will respond within 30 days.

### Children
This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

### Changes to this policy
We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above.
